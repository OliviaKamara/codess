What’s Included in Website Maintenance Services?

Codess website maintenance plans include ongoing updates, bug fixes, performance improvements, SEO updates, and technical support to keep your website running smoothly and ranking properly.

From small content updates to SEO optimization and performance tuning, these services are designed to help businesses maintain a strong, professional online presence without the stress of managing it alone.

Small updates include text edits, image replacements, link swaps, minor content-level adjustments, and other light maintenance tasks. New pages, redesigns, advanced animations, custom features, blog writing, full SEO campaigns, and major development work are billed separately unless otherwise agreed.
