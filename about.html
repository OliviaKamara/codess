ABOUT ME

Hi, I’m Olivia Kamara, a software engineering student at the TripleTen boot camp with a passion for design and the way things work. Coding gets my brain gears spinning—there’s nothing I love more than solving challenging problems and bringing creative ideas to life.

Before diving into web development, I built a strong background in marketing, graphic design, and business coaching, where I successfully helped small businesses grow through well-crafted marketing campaigns and thoughtful rebranding strategies. This experience honed my skills in creating user-centric designs, strategic planning, and communicating effectively—skills I now bring to the tech world as I build intuitive and visually appealing web applications.

Currently, I’m proficient in HTML, CSS, and front-end JavaScript, and I’m diving deeper into back-end technologies, frameworks, and honing my existing skills as I progress through my studies.

When I’m not coding, you’ll find me hiking on trails, volunteering in my community, or spending time with my daughter—my biggest inspiration. I believe in lifelong learning, creativity, and building solutions that make an impact. Let's connect, collaborate, and create something amazing together!
